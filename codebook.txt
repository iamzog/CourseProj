Below is a list of all the columns found in the dataset.
The subject column is each subject in the experiment
The activity column is one of 6 options (WALKING, WALKING_UPSTAIRS, WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS, SITTING, STANDING, LAYING)
The different measurements are represented in the remainder of the columns. Measurements may have an X, Y, or Z direction
The measurements are means [mean()] or standard deviantions [std()]
